Development of Deep Learning System for Analysis of Quantitative MRI of the Knee

  • Undergraduate research at North Carolina State University. Assisted in the development of a deep learning neural network in MATLAB capable of segmenting knee MRI images with 95-99% accuracy.
  • Achievement: Selected for highly competitive Abrams Scholar's Program and award stipend for high productivity and excellence in the lab.
  • Impact: Network was deployed to UNC Chapel Hill's Department of Exercise and Sports Science for use in research on the impact of ACL reconstruction surgery on articular cartilage.
